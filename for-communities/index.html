How we work together

Compass Place connects families with senior care communities that are the right fit. The more current your availability information, the better we can match families to your community , and the less time everyone spends on placements that don't work out.

We keep things simple. Most of what we need from you happens over text message , no apps, no logins, no portals.

Availability updates

A quick text keeps our data fresh and families informed

Each week , typically on Monday mornings , you'll receive a text from (888) 910-6866 asking for your current availability. Simply reply to that message with your counts for each care level you offer.

You can also send us an update any time something changes , a bed opens up, a waitlist clears, or your census shifts mid-week. Just text us the same format and we'll update your listing right away.

Example availability reply
AL2 MC-1

Care level codes

ILIndependent Living
ALAssisted Living
MCMemory Care
SNFSkilled Nursing: Female Semi-Private
SNMSkilled Nursing: Male Semi-Private
SNPSkilled Nursing , Private

How numbers work

  • +4
    A positive number means that many spots are currently available. Example: AL4 means 4 Assisted Living spots open.
  • 0
    A zero means that care level is currently full with no waitlist. Example: MC0 means Memory Care is full.
  • −3
    A negative number means that many families are on the waitlist. Example: SNF-3 means 3 families waiting for a Female Semi-Private Skilled Nursing bed.
You only need to send what changed. If your IL and AL counts are the same as last week, just send the care levels that are different. We'll keep the rest as-is. Partial updates are always accepted.

The text is case-insensitive , al4, AL4, and Al4 all work the same way.

Tour coordination

Simple text-based scheduling with no back-and-forth

When a family wants to tour your community, we'll send you a text with a tour request. You can reply with your available times, and we'll confirm with the family. Once a tour is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation text that includes a short tour code.

The tour code

Every confirmed tour has a unique 4-character code , a mix of letters and numbers, like jh43 or m7r2. This code ties all communication about that tour together so nothing gets lost.

  • Code
    Always 4 characters, any combination of letters and numbers. You'll see it in every message related to that tour.
Example tour time proposal
df54 3/22 9am

How a typical tour goes

1
We reach out

You receive a text from (888) 910-6866 with the family's name, care level interest, and preferred date range.

2
You reply with a proposed time

Text back the tour code followed by your proposed date and time (e.g. df54 3/22 9am). To decline, reply DECLINE. We will handle confirming with the family.

3
Tour confirmed

Once the family confirms, you'll get a final confirmation with the tour code, date, time, and the family contact's name.

4
If the family needs to reschedule

You will receive a new text with times the family is proposing. Reply the same way: tour code, date, and time. If none of the times work, reply DECLINE and we will keep working on it.

5
If you need to reschedule

Text us at (317) 426-6866 and a real person will help coordinate a new time with the family. We are working on a more direct solution for this and will update this guide when it is ready.

Family communication

See what families see , and how you fit in

Families use the Compass Place app to browse communities, compare fit, schedule tours, and communicate with our team. You won't need to use the app directly , but it helps to understand how the experience works on their end.

When a family sends a message or a tour gets scheduled, our team manages the coordination between both sides. You'll receive the relevant details over text. If a conversation thread has history you'd like to reference, we include a link in our texts that opens directly in your browser , no account required.

You're always in the loop. Every text we send you includes context about which family and which tour it relates to. You'll never wonder who's who , we include the resident's name, the family contact's name, and the tour code in every message.

Families may also leave reviews of your community after a tour or placement. Reviews are moderated by our team before they're published, and they factor into how your community appears to future families searching in your area.
