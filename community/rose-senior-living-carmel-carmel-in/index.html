Overview

Rose Senior Living Carmel is an Edward Rose & Sons community in Carmel's Arts and Design District offering Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Memory Care with the Heartfelt Connections program. The community features resort-style amenities including a rooftop lounge, billiards room, movie theater, full-service salon and spa, fitness center, atrium garden, and walking paths with pond and gazebo.
