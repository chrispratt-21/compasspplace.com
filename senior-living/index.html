Coverage grows market by market

Compass Place is being built to scale well beyond one county, but the public web structure is intentionally focused. That keeps the current branch useful and coherent instead of pretending every market is equally complete before it actually is.

Families may still see nearby communities in adjacent counties inside the app when those options are relevant to distance, but the live public senior-living branch is currently centered on Hamilton County.
